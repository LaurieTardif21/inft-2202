About the Animal List

This application allows users to manage a list of animals by adding, viewing, and listing them easily.

Built by Laurie Tardif for the INFT 2202 course. This project showcases modern web development practices, including the use of local storage, validation, and event handling with JavaScript.

We feature a wide variety of animals, including mammals, birds, and reptiles. Our collection includes both domestic and wild animals from various regions.

You can add an animal to our list by clicking on the "Animal" link in the navigation bar. There, you will be able to enter the animal's details.

Currently, we do not have a direct option to remove an animal from the list. However, you can contact us, and we will be able to help you.
